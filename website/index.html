An Entire Analytics Team in One Platform

BizRithm uses a multi-agent architecture to handle complex data tasks autonomously.

Multi-Agent Orchestration

The core brain of BizRithm. An intelligent router that delegates tasks to specialized sub-agents (SQL, Chat, Forecasting) for maximum accuracy and speed.

Conversational AI Chat

Chat directly with your business data. Powered by Gemini 1.5, the AI maintains session memory and provides context-aware strategic advice instantly.

NL to SQL Engine

Stop writing complex queries manually. Our agent safely translates natural language questions into executable PostgreSQL queries with strict data sanitization.

ML Forecasting Registry

Automatically predict future trends. The system evaluates 5 ML models (Prophet, XGBoost, Random Forest, etc.) to select the most accurate forecast for your metrics.

Automated Business Insights

The analytics engine automatically calculates KPIs, identifies seasonal trends, and flags statistical anomalies using advanced Z-score detection.

Live Interactive Dashboards

Stunning, responsive data visualizations built with Plotly and a premium dark glassmorphism UI. Track your performance across regions, products, and time.

PDF Report Generation

Generate comprehensive, board-ready PDF reports with a single click. Includes AI-generated executive summaries and high-impact strategic recommendations.

Data Upload & Management

Seamlessly ingest your business datasets via drag-and-drop. Supports CSV, Excel, and JSON formats with an integrated data explorer and statistical summaries.

How It Works

From raw data to actionable intelligence in 3 simple steps.

01

Upload Your Data

Drag and drop your CSV, Excel, or JSON files. The system automatically cleans, categorizes, and ingests your data into a secure PostgreSQL database.

02

AI Analyzes & Learns

The multi-agent orchestration layer kicks in. It calculates KPIs, detects anomalies, and trains machine learning models on your dataset in the background.

03

Discover & Decide

Explore the interactive dashboard, chat with the AI consultant, run future forecasts, and export professional reports to drive your business forward.
